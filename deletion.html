Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: June 3, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of data associated with Meta-connected apps, automations, and services operated by Rayan A’ Cader.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Remove the app from your Meta account settings to revoke access.
  2. Contact us through this GitHub profile: https://github.com/minutechreview.
  3. In your request, include enough information to identify the connected account so we can locate and delete relevant stored data.

What Happens Next

  • We will review the request and confirm the relevant account or data scope.
  • We will delete applicable stored data that is not required for legal, security, or operational reasons.
  • We may retain minimal records where necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

Alternative Option

You can also remove the app directly from your Meta settings at any time to stop future access.
